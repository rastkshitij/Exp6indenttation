Nano-Indentation Formulae

Contact Depth

hc = h − ε P S

where h is maximum indentation depth, P is maximum load, S is unloading stiffness, ε ≈ 0.75 (Berkovich indenter).

Projected Contact Area

A = f(hc)

Area function depends on indenter geometry.

Hardness

H = P A

Ratio of maximum load to projected contact area.

Elastic Modulus

E = √π 2 · S √A

Calculated using unloading stiffness method.
